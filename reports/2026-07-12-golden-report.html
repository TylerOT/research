VIII. What Would Prove This Wrong

Every view above dies at one price: Nasdaq above 30,976 or Dow above 53,824 ends the index short thesis; gold under 3,943 delays but does not cancel the metal thesis (destination unchanged, entry moves to 3,650-3,760); dollar index above 101.55 ends the currency thesis; copper above 6.76 ends its short. If the July 24-27 window passes with no stress at all, the September window inherits the full decision.
